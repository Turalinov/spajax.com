  • Lymphatic Drainage facial massage

    Lymphatic drainage facial massage is a rejuvenating and healing procedure to combat age-related changes. Massage accelerates the stagnation of lymph flow, removes harmful substances, and excess fluid. Lymphatic drainage massage sessions improve tissue metabolism, stimulate the production of elastin and collagen, restoring skin elasticity.

  • Chemical Peel

    A chemical peel is a powerful skin exfoliating solution applied to the face, neck, or hands to reveal the smooth and clear skin underneath. What makes this technique so popular is that it’s useful for a variety of skin concerns. At A&A Medical Spa we can offer different strength chemical peels from the very gentle stimulating peels that can give your skin a beautiful glow without any downtime to the strongest ones that can improve the texture, reduce scars and pigmentation.

  • Microneedling (aka Collagen Induction therapy)

    Microneedling is a cosmetic procedure that involves stimulating skin regeneration and introducing various anti-aging preparations. It helps to improve flabbiness of the skin, deep and superficial wrinkles, pigmentation, acne, the presence of post-acne, enlarged pores, stretch marks, scars, cellulite, hair loss.The microneedling procedure is aimed at rejuvenating the skin of the face and body.
